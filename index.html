Rivella was born Valerie Rivella Roth on the 25th Januray 1941.

From an early age she was into anything artistic. Theatre, drawing, painting, designing anything that was new and interesting as well as appearing in many theatrical productions and music videos.

She used to belong to a radio club and sent most of her earlier works to them for competitions, then in 1950 entered the Tara Girls School in Paramatta, NSW, art show that was being judged by a well known artist.

Rivella won a Highly Commended prize for her painting of the school - done with a broken collar bone.

At the age of 13 she was offered a job with Hanna Barbara to be sent to Disney Studios in the USA to study, then return and work for Hanna Barbara - but her father would not let her go.
